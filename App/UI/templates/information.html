Top Tracks

There is no way to see your most listened to tracks without using Spotify Wrapped, which can only be seen at end of the year. This feature is provided by TopTracks, which allows you to generate a playlist of your favorite music from the previous month, six months, or all time. You may also choose to have these playlists updated automatically so that they always include your favorite songs. You may disable automatic updates in Spotify simply removing the playlist.

Create

Soulify offers a unique playlist creation tool that mixes music based on tunable attributes. To begin, select up to 5 song titles or artists that you wish to be featured in the playlist. Name your playlist and then set the tunable attributes that you wish to emphasize or eliminate from your playlist.  The resulting playlist will be unaffected by non-selected qualities.
