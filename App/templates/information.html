Create

This site provides you with several features to find new music and create playlist in an easy fashion. First, enter up to 5 song names or artists that you want the playlist to reflect. Second, set the tuneable attributes that you want to highlight in your playlist. This includes attributes such as energy, which measures the intensity of music, and valence, which describes the mood of the track. Non-selected attributes will have no effect on the generated playlist.
